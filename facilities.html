Facilities

There are three well-appointed lounges with colour televisions. One of them is a nice quiet upstairs lounge for residents who want to read or entertain their relatives. The other two lounges are downstairs, near the carers’ station and the office so the staff can keep an eye on their wellbeing and attend to their needs. There is a water dispenser in the main lounge for the residents and relatives to use.

There is a separate dining room, retaining a lot of the original features of the property, where residents who are able or who want to socialise can have their meals. There are always members of staff handy to help feed residents who are unable to feed themselves.

The back garden and patio are there for the residents to enjoy as an extra space. The Gardening Club meets there in the summer months. The building and the gardens have been specially adapted to allow for easy and safe access. The Home is equipped with all specialised equipment for the benefit of the residents and is fully maintained.


Bedrooms

The Home has 22 single rooms, some with en-suite facilities. All the rooms have been refurbished to very high specifications with new fitted wardrobes, vanity units, lockable bedside cabinets, shelves, chest of drawers and new carpets, curtains, bedding, and light fittings.


Activites

We employ two Activities co-ordinators who organise different activities based on resident preferences. The activities range from bingo sessions, card games, dominoes, baking and gardening. They also take different residents out for shopping trips on a rotational basis. There are trips arranged to garden centres, local parks, and a local pub for lunch. In the summer, the residents can be taken slightly further afield. Relatives are most welcome to join their loved ones to any of the trips.

We also have outside entertainers to come and entertain our residents. This can include a singer for a sing-along, magic show, or a comedy show. Summer Fairs, coffee mornings, Easter Bonnet competition and events to mark special occasions and special birthdays are also held at Silver Birch. Residents and Relatives are involved in the planning and organising these events.

Any money raised at these events goes into the Residents’ leisure fund for their benefit.
