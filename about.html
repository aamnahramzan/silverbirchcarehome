Who We Are

Silver Birch Care Home has been run as a family business, who are actively involved in the business, and work alongside the Manager and other staff to uphold the warm, friendly environment and exceptional level of care that has and will continue to be the staple of this home. We aim to improve the Quality of Life for the elderly generation, as we strive to meet the individual needs of each resident. We achieve and maintain this high standard, by helping them in their daily’s lives, both psychologically and physically. Keeping our residents as active as their health permits enables them to lead better and fuller lives.

At Silver Birch, we understand that there are numerous difficulties in this process and a whole host of emotional decisions to be made, that’s why we provide as much detail as possible when assisting you or your loved one with the transition – especially when it comes to choosing the right care home to best suit your requirements.

We provide tailor made care packages with a range of services in our residential care home, with a high level of support and expertise provided throughout. Our main aim is to provide high quality care for the residents we look after. We follow our philosophy of care with “Privacy, Dignity and Respect.”

Creating a warm and friendly environment is a key part of our philosophy here at Silver Birch, ensuring we treat everyone with sensitivity and respect.


What is the Philosophy of Care?


  • Enhance the quality of life by providing a safe, secure, comfortable and supportive environment.
  • Ensure that the dignity of each Resident is maintained at all times.
  • Provide a secure, stable and comfortable environment whilst providing a standard of individual psychological and physical care.
  • Enhance the quality of life by providing a safe, secure, comfortable and supportive environment.
  • You will be treated with respect and dignity at all times
  • Maintain each resident’s right to have their political, religious and emotional needs accepted and respected.
